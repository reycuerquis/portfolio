About

Are you in pain, physical or emotional? Do you feel like you are not being all you can be - that something is blocking you from a happy, healthy, fulfilled life? I can help.

I believe that mind, body and spirit work together, and to get the root causes of any pain, be it physical or emotional, I need to look at the whole person, not the outer sypmtoms. This enables lasting, all pervasive changes for my clients.

As well as being a qualified RTT Therapist/Hypnotherapist, I also transform the body, as well as the mind, through Kinesiolgy. Bowen Therapy/Neurostructural Integration (NST), Inegrated Complimentary Medicines, and Life Coaching. I have now been a practitioner for many years and have seen so many lives transformed.

Do give me a call, or email me, so that we can set up a free phone or zoom consultation, and we can organise your first transforming RTT session. I am so looking forward to hearing from you.
